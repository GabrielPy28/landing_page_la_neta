Program Details

To qualify, you must:

  • Be 18 years or older
  • Reside in the United States
  • Have 100,000+ followers on TikTok, YouTube, or another social platform
  • Create original short-form videos (under 90 seconds)
  • Have (or create) a Facebook Page and a professional Instagram account, and link them together
  • Follow Meta's content and monetization policies

Reels
Longer videos

You can earn up to $5,000 in Breakthrough Bonuses in your first 90 days, plus additional earnings from standard Facebook Content Monetization (Reels, ads on videos, etc.).
